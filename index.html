Sigma Lambda Beta | Celebrating 30 years of brotherhood!

After three decades, Sigma Lambda Beta has grown to over 100 collegiate entities spanning across 29 states from Coast to Coast. Moreover, our membership has expanded beyond our Hispanic-Latino origins making us one of the most culturally diverse Greek lettered organizations.

Mighty Xi | 2015 - 2016 award recipients for fraternity of the year at UIC

The UIC Sigma Lambda Beta Chapter has proven the test of time by continuing to grow and strive with the help of men from all walks of life. We continue nurture and further a dynamic, values-based environment which utilizes our historically Latino based fraternity as a catalyst to better serve the needs and wants of all people.

Strong commitment to

Brotherhood

We view the bond between each member as an immeasurable symbol of trust, respect, and most importantly, love.

Scholarship

Academic excellence is one of our top priorities. We ensure that every one of our brothers is achieving their highest potential by providing academic resources for all of our members.

Community Service

Our members excel in their motivation and dedication to give back to the world. Throughout the year, we dedicate many of our events to fundraise for different causes.

Cultural Awareness

Our experiences, values, and cultural background lead every individual to be just as unique as the next one. We take pride in having one of the most culturally diverse fraternities, and it is important to us to share our cultures with the world.
